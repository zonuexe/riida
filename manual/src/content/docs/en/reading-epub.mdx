<Aside type="caution" title="Feature in development">
The EPUB viewer is currently in development. Basic reading works, but there are known issues with link handling.
</Aside>

## Basic Navigation

### Page Controls

| Action | Method |
|--------|--------|
| Next page | <kbd>→</kbd> / <kbd>PageDown</kbd> |
| Previous page | <kbd>←</kbd> / <kbd>PageUp</kbd> |

### Reading Position

Your position (<abbr title="Canonical Fragment Identifier">CFI</abbr>) is saved automatically when you close the viewer and restored on next open.

## Viewer Settings

Click the settings icon in the toolbar to adjust font size, background color, and layout.

## Known Issues

### Link Handling

Links inside EPUB content do not work correctly in this version:

- External links (`https://`): clicking may produce no response
- Same-section anchor links (`#anchor`): clicking may break the page layout

This is caused by cross-frame DOM interaction issues in macOS WKWebView and is under investigation.

### Other Limitations

- Complex CSS or custom font declarations may cause layout issues
- Image-heavy EPUBs may be slow to load
